How to Play




Hold B1VS, earn BNB

Buy some B1VS, and start earning BNB dividends! Your earnings are proportionate to the percentage of B1VS supply you hold.



Reinvest your Earnings

Get more of the supply percentage in your grasp by reinvesting your BNB dividends, to get more B1VS shares!



Sell & Withdraw Anytime

When you're ready to withdraw BNB, simply sell some B1VS shares and withdraw - It's easy and possible anytime!

More than an Hourglass

Function Island makes B1VS a truly unique experience




Passive BNB Yield

B1VS can be used as a store of value, and as a representation of value transfer - you can send and receive B1VS to any user on Binance Smart Chain, using their BEP-20 Address.



Gamified Savings

Function Island B1VS uses the "Proof of Weak Hands" game theory to enable a highly volatile but rewarding ecosystem, engineered for the most strong-handed users!



Always Extendable

More Dapps, contracts & protocols can always be connected to the B1VS Hourglass contract to extend its functionality, or to benefit from it in some way, shape or form.
