B1VS Hourglass

A classic Proof of Weak Hands game

1: Get some B1VS

Buy some B1VS shares with BNB. When you do this, you'll incur a 10% Dividend Fee, paid out to existing B1VS holders. The more B1VS shares you hold, the larger percentage of the dividends you are paid.


2: Refer Others to Play

Share your referral link around to friends, family and fellow degens - when they use it to buy some B1VS shares, you'll earn a bonus of 3% of their total deposit, as BNB dividends! Spread the word about B1VS Hourglass and enjoy!


3: Compound your Earnings

Reinvesting your dividends is the fastest way to earn more in this game - doing this every day is almost akin to "compound interest". Are you a godly chad investor?


4: Sell B1VS & Withdraw BNB

When you're done earning dividends, you can sell your B1VS shares and withdraw the underlying collateralised BNB. Don't worry, you can join in again and start earning any time!


B1VS Gauntlet

How to be a Godly Chad Stronghand!

1: Create your Gauntlet

Choose a number of days to lock your B1VS away for. Don't worry, the Gauntlet does not affect your everyday B1VS - you'll be depositing BNB to get B1VS for the gauntlet in step 2!


2: Get B1VS with BNB

Add some B1VS to your Gauntlet by depositing BNB - your Gauntlet will buy B1VS from the Hourglass contract for you, and lock them away until your set day!


3: Compound your Earnings

Reinvesting your Gauntlet dividends is the fastest way to earn more in this game - doing this every day is almost akin to "compound interest". Show your strong hands with Gauntlet mode!


4: Sell B1VS & Withdraw BNB

When the Gauntlet lock-time has elapsed, you'll be able to sell the B1VS within it and withdraw BNB to your wallet! Of course, you also have the option to add more days to your Gauntlet, and start another cycle!
